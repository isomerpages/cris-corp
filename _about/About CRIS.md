<p>The Consortium for Clinical Research and Innovation, Singapore (CRIS)
was established in 2020 with the goal of strengthening synergies and developing
strategies for national-level clinical research and translation programmes
that are under the stewardship of the Ministry of Health. It is a subsidiary
of MOH Holdings (MOHH).</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Resources_Media/2022/220406_CRIS%20symposium/CRIS01.jpg">
</div>
<p>CRIS brings together seven <a href="/news-and-events/media-releases/220406-CRIS-launch/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">key national R&amp;D initiatives and clinical services</a>,
and facilitate synergistic collaborations among them. These initiatives
include:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><a href="/our-programmes/scri/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Singapore Clinical Research Institute</a> (SCRI)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/our-programmes/nhic/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">National Health Innovation Centre Singapore</a> (NHIC)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/our-programmes/actris/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Advanced Cell Therapy and Research Institute, Singapore</a> (ACTRIS)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/our-programmes/precise/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Precision Health Research, Singapore</a> (PRECISE)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/our-programmes/stcc/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Singapore Translational Cancer Consortium</a> (STCC)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/our-programmes/cadence/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Cardiovascular Disease National Collaborative Enterprise</a> (CADENCE)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>SIngapore Medical Foundation AI Model (SIMFONI)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>CRIS aims to make a positive difference to Singapore patients and researchers
by ensuring that these clinical research platforms and programmes are at
the cutting edge of capability development and innovation. This will be
achieved through facilitated collaborations and enduring partnerships with
research and biomedical entities and communities across the public sector
and industry across Singapore.</p>
