<p>The Singapore Translational Cancer Consortium (STCC) is a nationally-coordinated
consortium to synergise cancer research capabilities in Singapore.</p>
<p>Established in 2020, STCC brings together unmatched basic, clinical and
translational talent in&nbsp;Singapore to create globally significant peaks
of excellence in selected Asian cancers.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Corporate%20photos/07%20-%20STCC%202.png">
</div>
<p>STCC’s&nbsp;joint platforms provide an enabling research and innovation
environment driven to foster translational research with meaningful outcomes
for society. These platforms are:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Clinical Trials and Investigational Medicine Units</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cancer Databases and Tissue Banks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Translational Research Integration and Support</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Business Intelligence and Development</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Impact and Population Health</p>
</li>
</ul>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Corporate%20photos/06%20-%20STCC%201.png">
</div>
<p>Through these forged collaborative relationships between local cancer
research groups and by capitalising on the strengths of industry and academia,
STCC is uniquely poised to develop initiatives&nbsp;that are aligned with
Singapore’s goals in value-based healthcare innovation and economic value
creation.</p>
<p>For more information, please visit the <a href="https://stcc.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">STCC website</a>.</p>
