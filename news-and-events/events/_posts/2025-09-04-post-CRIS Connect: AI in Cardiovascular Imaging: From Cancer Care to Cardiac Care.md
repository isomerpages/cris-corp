<h4><strong>About the Speakers</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Dr Sia Ching Hui</strong>
<br><em>Consultant<br>Department of Cardiology, National University Heart Centre, Singapore (NUHCS)</em>
</p>
<p><em>Adjunct Assistant Professor<br>Yong Loo Lin School of Medicine, National University of Singapore (NUS)</em>
</p>
<p>Dr Sia Ching Hui is a Cardiologist at the Department of Cardiology in
National University Heart Centre, Singapore (NUHCS). Concurrently, he has
an Adjunct Assistant Professor appointment at Yong Loo Lin School of Medicine,
National University of Singapore. He holds Master’s degrees in internal
medicine, clinical investigation and business administration.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Dr Sia specialises in Multi-Modality Cardiac Imaging (Echocardiography,
Nuclear Cardiology, Computed Tomography, Cardiovascular Magnetic Resonance)
and Cardio-Neurology (Brain-Heart Interactions). He has been actively involved
in research with more than 300 papers in peer-reviewed journals and high
impact journals. He has also been awarded multiple research grants, including
the National Medical Research Council's Transition Award.</p>
<p>Dr Sia is a member of the <a href="https://www.cadence-cvd.sg" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Cardiovascular Disease National Collaborative Enterprise (CADENCE)</a> Executive
Committee. He also serves as a Specialty Editor for the Singapore Medical
Journal.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Ms Kok Su Ing</strong>
</p>
<p>Clinical Research Coordinator
<br><em>Department of Cardiology, National University Heart Centre, Singapore (NUHCS)</em>
</p>
<p>Ms Kok Su Ing is a clinical research coordinator at National University
Heart Centre, Singapore (NUHCS). She holds a Bachelor’s degree in Biological
Sciences from Nanyang Technology University (NTU) and a Diploma in Biomedical
Science from Singapore Polytechnic.</p>
<p>Ms Kok has over seven years of experience in research trial coordination.
Her role entails overseeing daily operations of multiple clinical trials
and observational studies. She has supported study start-ups, recruitments,
participant screenings, and coordinated meetings between stakeholders and
vendors for local studies.</p>
<p>Ms Kok is passionate about advancing research and research management.</p>
