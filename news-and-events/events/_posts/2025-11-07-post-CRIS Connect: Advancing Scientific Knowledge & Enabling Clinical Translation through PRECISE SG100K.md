<h4><strong>About the Speakers</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Dr Janice Goh</strong>
<br><em>Senior Scientist<br>Bioinformatics Institute, A*STAR</em>
</p>
<p>Dr Janice Goh is the project lead for the PRECISE-100K flagship project
titled “An Integrated Pharmacoeconomic-Pharmacokinetic Framework for Prioritizing
and Testing Clinically Important Drug-Gene Interactions”.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Dr Goh is a registered pharmacist with the Singapore Pharmacy Council,
who completed her PhD in the lab of Professor Rada Savic at the UCSF School
of Pharmacy. Her work in Bioinformatics Institute, A*STAR, focuses on applying
translational pharmacometrics tools by capitalizing on preclinical data
to predict clinical outcomes prior to actual trials.</p>
<p><strong>A/Prof Yeo See Cheng</strong>
</p>
<p><em>Head of Department<br>Renal Medicine</em>
</p>
<p><em>Clinical Director<br>Data Analytics Management and Research, Tan Tock Seng Hospital</em>
</p>
<p><em>Associate Professor<br>Lee Kong Chian School of Medicine</em>
</p>
<p>A/Prof Yeo See Cheng is the project lead for the PRECISE-SG100K flagship
project titled “Unravelling the Determinants of Kidney Health in a Multi-Ethnic
Asian Population”.</p>
<p>A/Prof Yeo is Senior Consultant &amp; Head at the Department of Renal
Medicine of Tan Tock Seng Hospital. His research and clinical interest
is in chronic kidney disease and glomerular disease.&nbsp;</p>
<p>A/Prof Yeo is also a recipient of NMRC HPHSR Clinician Scientist Award,
Singapore Society of Nephrology Achievement Award and NHG Health Outstanding
Citizenship Award for his research and clinical work. He has published
in several high impact peer-reviewed journals and participates actively
in clinical trials.</p>
<p>
<br><strong>Dr Huang Huiping</strong>
</p>
<p><em>Manager<br>Precision Health Research Singapore (PRECISE), Consortium for Clinical Research and Innovation, Singapore (CRIS)</em>
</p>
<p>With a PhD in Drug Discovery from the University of Genoa, Italy, Dr Huang
Huiping brings strong drug discovery research experience across academia
and industry, with expertise in neuroscience and in vivo pharmacology.
In her current role at <a href="www.npm.sg" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Precision Health Research, Singapore (PRECISE)</a>,
she drives genomic innovation initiatives to deliver public health impact.</p>
<p>Prior to joining PRECISE, Dr Huang has worked in medical affairs and business
development roles within the life sciences sector, bridging scientific
innovation with commercial strategy.&nbsp;</p>
<p>She is passionate about innovative technologies, fostering collaborations
and knowledge exchange.</p>
